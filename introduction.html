Introduction

Welcome, Future Ecologist! Did you know that ecosystems cover every part of our planet, from the massive Sahara Desert to a tiny puddle in your backyard? Earth is a collection of diverse biomes where living and non-living things constantly interact.

Every plant, animal, and microorganism in a habitat plays a specific role to maintain ecological balance. If one level of the food chain is disrupted, the whole ecosystem can become unbalanced.

Your goal in this activity is to analyze how ecosystems work, identify their components (biotic and abiotic factors), and design a preservation guide for one endangered biome. Ready to start?
