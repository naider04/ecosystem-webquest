Evaluation Rubric

Review the expectations below to make sure your infographic qualifies for a high score. Your final project will be evaluated using this rubric:

Criteria Excellent (4 pts) Satisfactory (2.5 pts) Needs Improvement (1 pt)
Ecosystem Research & Food Chains Accurately classifies the biotope (abiotic factors) and biocenosis, identifying correct food chain roles (producers, consumers, and scavengers). Identifies most parts of the ecosystem and food chain, but contains minor errors in classification or ecological roles. Incomplete classification of the food chain; incorrect scientific parameters or missing biological roles.
Problem Solving (Human Impact & Solution) Analyzes a realistic environmental threat to the chosen biome and proposes a logical, actionable conservation solution. Identifies an environmental threat but the proposed solution lacks depth, feasibility, or logical connection. Fails to address human impact or does not propose a relevant solution to protect the biome.
CLIL & Language Integration (English) Communication is clear and coherent; vocabulary is accurate with terms like 'deciduous', 'spines', 'biotope', or 'nutrients' used properly. Zero spelling errors. Communication is meaningful and understandable; contains minor grammar or spelling errors that do not hinder comprehension. Frequent language and grammatical errors make it difficult to follow or understand.
Visual Design & Layout (Canva) The infographic design is highly engaging, visually balanced, organized, and clean. It is easy to read and navigate. The design is generally organized and clear, with minor alignment or spacing issues. Disorganized presentation, cluttered layout, or poor choices of color and font make it hard to read.
